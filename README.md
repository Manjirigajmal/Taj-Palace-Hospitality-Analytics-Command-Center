# 🏨 Taj Palace Hospitality Analytics Command Center

## Overview

The **Taj Palace Hospitality Analytics Command Center** is an end-to-end Power BI solution developed as part of the **KSR Datavizon Hospitality Analytics Challenge**.

The objective of this project is to transform hospitality operational data into actionable business insights that support executive decision-making across revenue management, guest experience, distribution channels, hotel operations, and loyalty programs.

The dashboard analyzes hospitality data from **2021–2025** and provides a centralized view of business performance across multiple cities, hotel categories, booking channels, managers, and customer segments.

---

## Business Problem

Hospitality organizations generate large volumes of operational and customer data. However, decision-makers often struggle to consolidate information across revenue, guest satisfaction, booking channels, and loyalty programs.

This project addresses the challenge by providing:

* Portfolio-wide performance visibility
* Revenue and profitability analysis
* Hotel manager performance benchmarking
* Booking channel effectiveness analysis
* Guest experience monitoring
* Loyalty member analytics
* Executive-level decision support

## Key Insights

### Revenue

* Heritage properties emerged as the largest revenue contributors.
* Udaipur generated the highest revenue among all cities.
* Direct booking channels delivered the strongest revenue performance.

### Guest Experience

* Guest satisfaction remained consistently high across the portfolio.
* Premium categories demonstrated stronger guest experience ratings.
* Top-performing properties established benchmarks for service excellence.

### Loyalty Program

* Loyalty members contributed a significant share of bookings and revenue.
* Opportunities exist to increase member spending and engagement.
* Member retention remains a key growth driver.

### Channel Performance

* Website bookings generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Mobile devices accounted for the majority of booking activity.
* Direct digital acquisition channels outperformed intermediary channels.

---

## Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategic recommendations are proposed:

1. Expand loyalty acquisition and engagement programs.
2. Increase investment in high-performing direct booking channels.
3. Replicate successful practices from top-performing cities and properties.
4. Improve mobile booking experiences to align with customer behavior.
5. Leverage guest experience insights to increase repeat visitation and customer lifetime value.
